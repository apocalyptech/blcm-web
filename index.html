Hello Vault Hunter!

This page aims to be the first stop for folks interested in running mods for the Borderlands series (primarily for Borderlands 2 + The Pre-Sequel, at the moment). It's maintained by the folks at Shadow's Evil Hideout, so stop by there if you have any questions or suggestions about the site.

The links along the top should get you started with preparing your Borderlands games for modding, finding mods to use, and running them. Below, you'll find any notable larger events which have happened in the modding community (starting around April 2019). Click through for more information on those items as they show up, and subscribe to the RSS feed if you use an RSS reader!
